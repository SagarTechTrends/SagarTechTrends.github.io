About Me

From analyzing large datasets to deploying AI-powered apps, my journey has been about making data work smarter and more impactful. I’m passionate about Large Language Models (LLMs), Data Analytics, and AI applications that go beyond numbers tools that help people ask better questions, uncover clearer insights, and drive smarter decisions. I’ve built projects such as a NYC Property Price Predictor using PySpark, XGBoost, and SHAP, and a Streamlit app for LLMs in Business Intelligence, where I gained hands-on experience with predictive modeling, natural language query generation, and interactive analytics. While I’m still expanding my expertise in LLMs, NLP, and AI model deployment, I’m deeply motivated to explore emerging areas like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), embeddings, and multimodal AI with the goal of applying these technologies to solve real-world business challenges.
